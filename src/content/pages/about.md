Robin Xie works at the intersection of software, capital, media, and identity. The through-line is not a job title but a method: understand the system, find the leverage, build the interface that makes it legible.

She came up through engineering and operating work, then expanded into executive leadership, investing, and long-horizon thinking about how AI rewires value creation. She moves between product detail and macro structure, between what ships this quarter and what compounds over a decade.

The site is a calm control surface for those pursuits. Books stay alive. Projects stay curated. Writing stays sharp. The goal is coherence, not volume.
